Admissions **Schedule**
=======================

CUI lahore campus offers admissions twice a year; in Fall and Spring. Fall admissions tentatively commence in June and spring admissions tentatively commence in October/ November of each Year.

Fall 2025 admissions are now open for both graduate and undergraduate programs.

Admission Schedule for Fall 2025 is as follows:

Description Schedule
Admissions Open Sunday, June 01, 2025
Application Deadline Extended Monday, July 21, 2025
Entry Test

[Click here for Detail](https://lahore.comsats.edu.pk/admissions/admissions-test.aspx)July 13, 2025 (Deadline to apply: July 03, 2025) , 

 July 27-28, 2025 (Deadline to apply: July 17, 2025)
Display of Merit List Wednesday, Aug 06, 2025
Commencement of Classes Monday, Sep 01, 2025
